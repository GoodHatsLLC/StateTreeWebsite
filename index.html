StateTree

Model your domain – not just its UI

Easy to Learn

Modelled on SwiftUI and with a near 1:1 concept mapping, StateTree will have you up and running in no time. There's no Redux here.

Top Tier Reactivity

StateTree frees the best-in-class reactive tooling patterns from UI. It is data driven and supports arbitrary deep linking and state replay by default.

Focus on What Matters

Write, run, and test your app's business logic independent of its UI. Expect a familiar but powerful environment with dependency injection and deterministic testing hooks.
